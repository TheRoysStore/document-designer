This guide was designed to help those who want to use rich text formatting with Document Designer.

One of the rich text items has already been set up for you- the em item.

To use it, surround your text with <em> and </em> tags.

You can also make text bold and italic. The <b>bold text</b> tags make text bold, and the <i>italic text</i> tags make text italic.

You can also make text bold and italic. The bold text tags make text bold, and the italic text tags make text italic.


In addition to all of this, you can also use built-in classes with document designer. Surround your text with span items, and specify the class.
